## Playing Card Image Prompts — Aces + Face Cards

Each section below contains prompts for one deck style. All decks need 16 images:
4 Aces + 4 Jacks + 4 Queens + 4 Kings.

**Shared rules for all decks:**
- Ratio: 2:3 (poker card proportions)
- Corner pips (rank letter + suit symbol in top-left / bottom-right) are added
  programmatically — do NOT include them in the AI image
- Leave clean white/solid margins at the top-left and bottom-right corners
  (at least 20% width × 22% height) so programmatic pips aren't obscured
- Center the main artwork on the card
---



# Modern Deck

Flat geometric vector art. Bold shapes, clean edges, no gradients. Minimalist color palette — pure red (#E53935) for red suits, deep navy (#1A237E) for black suits, on crisp white. Inspired by Swiss design / Bauhaus. Abstract geometric interpretations of royal figures.

**Structure rules:**
- Face cards use abstract geometric figure compositions (not traditional mirrored)
- Aces feature a single bold geometric suit symbol
- Flat color fills only, no shading, no texture
- Do NOT draw corner pips — they are added programmatically- Flat, Swiss Bauhaus-inspired composition
- Sharp clean edges, angular polygons
