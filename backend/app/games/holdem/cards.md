## Playing Cards — Aces + Face Cards (Line Art)

Clean line art on a white background, suitable for printing on a standard poker-size deck of cards. Each face card illustration is mirrored top-and-bottom in traditional playing card style (half-length figure, cut at the waist, with an inverted duplicate below). Crisp ink outlines with fine crosshatching for detail. Strictly monochrome — red suits use only red ink, black suits use only black ink. No gold, no color fill, no shading, no grayscale. Pure linework on white.

**Style reference:** Traditional engraved playing card illustration — ornate baroque scrollwork and filigree throughout. All cards should share the same visual language: fine crosshatching, decorative flourishes, and classical European court figure styling. No Celtic, Art Deco, or modern fantasy elements.

**Structure rules:**
- All face cards MUST use traditional mirrored/rotational symmetry (top half and inverted bottom half, cut at the waist)
- No full-body portraits, no feet visible, no animals or companions beside the figure
- No border frames on Aces; thin single-line border on face cards
- Corner pips: rank letter top-left and inverted bottom-right, with suit pip beneath the letter
