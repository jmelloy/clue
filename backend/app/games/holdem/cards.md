## Playing Card Image Prompts — Aces + Face Cards

Each section below contains prompts for one deck style. All decks need 16 images:
4 Aces + 4 Jacks + 4 Queens + 4 Kings.

**Shared rules for all decks:**
- Ratio: 2:3 (poker card proportions)
- Corner pips (rank letter + suit symbol in top-left / bottom-right) are added
  programmatically — do NOT include them in the AI image
- Leave clean white/solid margins at the top-left and bottom-right corners
  (at least 20% width × 22% height) so programmatic pips aren't obscured
- Center the main artwork on the card

# Classic Deck

Clean line art on a white background. Traditional engraved playing card style — ornate baroque scrollwork and filigree. Fine crosshatching for detail. Strictly monochrome — red suits use only red ink, black suits use only black ink. No gold, no color fill, no shading, no grayscale. Pure linework on white.

**Structure rules:**
- Face cards MUST use traditional mirrored/rotational symmetry (top half and inverted bottom half, cut at the waist)
- No full-body portraits, no feet visible, no animals or companions beside the figure

- Do NOT draw corner pips — they are added programmatically
